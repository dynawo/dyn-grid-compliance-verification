    \subsection{Step response to control changes: V-setpoint increase}

    Checks for compliant behavior of the generating unit under a grid scenario where there
    is a $5\%$ step increase in the setpoint of the primary voltage control.

    \GridCircuitZone

    \begin{description}
        \item Initial conditions used at the PDR bus:
        \begin{itemize}
            \item $P = P_\text{max\_unite}$
            \item $Q = 0$
            \item $U = U_\text{dim}$
            \item $SCR = 3$
        \end{itemize}
    \end{description}
