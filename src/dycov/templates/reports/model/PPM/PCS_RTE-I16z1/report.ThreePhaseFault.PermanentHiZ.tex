    \subsection{Permanent fault: High-impedance fault ($SCR=10, V=0.5 U_{dim}$)}

    Checks for compliant behavior of the generating unit under a grid scenario where there
    is a high-impedance three-phase permanent fault to ground, causing a voltage dip of 0.5 $U_{dim}$.

    \GridCircuitZoneFault

    \begin{description}
        \item Initial conditions used at the PDR bus:
        \begin{itemize}
            \item $P = P_\text{max\_unite}$
            \item $Q = 0$
            \item $U = U_\text{dim}$
            \item $SCR = 10$
        \end{itemize}
    \end{description}
